# AuditorReadOnly

## Who this role is for
Auditors who need read-only access to compliance and audit information.

## What you see in the app
- Reports and audit data are available at the API layer.
- UI visibility depends on navigation configuration in your deployment.

## Key responsibilities
- Review compliance summaries and overdue items.
- Review audit logs without making changes.

## Common actions and how to do them

### View compliance reports
1) Open `Reports` from the sidebar if available.
2) Review compliance summary, overdue assignments, and overdue reviews.

### Review audit logs
1) Open `Audit & Logs` if available.
2) Filter by policy or time range to inspect actions.

## Limits
- Read-only access; no policy creation, edits, or approvals.
- If reports or audit pages are not visible, request access from your admin.
