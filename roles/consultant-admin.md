# ConsultantAdmin

## Who this role is for
External consultants or platform admins who operate like OrgAdmins with approval capabilities.

## What you see in the app
- All OrgAdmin pages.
- `Policies to Approve` (approval queue).

## Key responsibilities
- Manage policies and org settings for client organizations.
- Approve or reject policy submissions.
- Provide compliance oversight.

## Common actions and how to do them

### Manage policies and approvals
1) Use `Policies` to create, edit, and submit policies.
2) Use `Policies to Approve` to review and decide on submissions.
3) Export approved policies from `Exports`.

## Limits
- You can view templates but only `SuperAdmin` can create, edit, or delete templates.
