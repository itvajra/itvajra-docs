# OrgUser

## Who this role is for
Everyday staff who must read and acknowledge policies assigned to them.

## What you see in the app
- Sidebar shows `Policies to Acknowledge`.
- You can open policy detail pages from your assignments.

## Key responsibilities
- Review assigned policies by their due date.
- Acknowledge policies to confirm understanding.
- Track what you have completed.

## Common actions and how to do them

### Acknowledge a policy
1) Open `Policies to Acknowledge`.
2) Select a policy from the list.
3) Review the policy content.
4) Click `Acknowledge`.

### Acknowledge a policy with a due date
1) Open `Policies to Acknowledge`.
2) Use the quick filter cards to focus on `Late` or `Soon`.
3) Open the policy detail view.
4) Confirm the due date and requirements.
5) Click `Acknowledge` to complete.

### View your acknowledgment history
1) Open `Policies to Acknowledge`.
2) Switch to the `Library` or completed view.
3) Filter by category, business unit, or search terms.

### Use filters and sorting effectively
1) Use the search bar to filter by title or content.
2) Toggle content search when needed.
3) Filter by category and business unit for focused lists.
4) Sort by due date or acknowledgment date to prioritize work.

### Find a policy quickly
1) Use the search bar for title or content.
2) Apply category or business unit filters.
3) Sort by due date or status.

### View policy details
1) Open `Policies to Acknowledge`.
2) Click the policy row or title.
3) Review the policy content and metadata.
4) Use the history or details panel if available.

## Limits
- You cannot create, edit, approve, or export policies.
- You do not access Templates, Reports, or Audit logs.
