# SuperAdmin

## Who this role is for
Platform administrators with full control across organizations and templates.

## What you see in the app
- All OrgAdmin pages.
- `Policies to Approve` (approval queue).
- Full template management (create, edit, delete).

## Key responsibilities
- Manage policy templates for all customers.
- Oversee approvals and policy lifecycle decisions.
- Support onboarding and compliance workflows.

## Common actions and how to do them

### Create or update a template
1) Open `Templates`.
2) Click `Create Template` or edit an existing template.
3) Save changes to publish the template.

### Approve policy submissions
1) Open `Policies to Approve`.
2) Review a policy and decide.
3) Confirm approval or request changes.

## Limits
- None within PolicyHQ features.
