# Troubleshooting

## I cannot see a page or action

- Verify your role permits the action.
- Ask an Org Admin to confirm your role and business units.

## A policy is missing

- Check the correct tab (Pending, Library, History).
- Confirm the policy is not retired or archived.
- Ensure you are assigned to the correct business unit.

## Import errors

- Fix rows marked as Error in the preview.
- Ensure role values match allowed roles.
- Check that business unit names are spelled correctly.

## Sign-in issues

- Confirm you are using the correct Microsoft account.
- Clear your browser cache and try again.
- Contact your admin if the account is not invited.
