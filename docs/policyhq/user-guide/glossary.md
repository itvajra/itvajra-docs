# Glossary

- Acknowledgement: a user confirms they have read and understood a policy
- Approval: decision by an approver to accept or send back a policy
- Business Unit: organizational group used to scope policies and users
- Draft: editable policy not yet submitted for approval
- Library: policies available for read-only access without acknowledgement
- Pending Approval: policy submitted and waiting on approver decision
- Pending Review: policy due for scheduled review
- Policy Template: reusable base used to generate policies
- Policy Version: a saved snapshot created each time a policy is updated
- Review Cycle: scheduled interval for policy review
- Tenant: your organization within PolicyHQ
