# Getting Started

## Sign in

1) Go to the PolicyHQ URL provided by your admin.
2) Select `Sign in` and complete Microsoft Entra ID login.
3) After login, you land on the Start or Landing page.

## First-time setup (if prompted)

Some organizations require onboarding before you can use the app.

1) Open `Onboarding` when prompted.
2) Provide the required organization details.
3) Submit the form to unlock the rest of the application.

## What you see after login

Your view depends on your role:

- Org Users see assigned policies and acknowledgements.
- Approvers see pending approvals and decision actions.
- Admins and Authors see policy, template, and org management screens.
