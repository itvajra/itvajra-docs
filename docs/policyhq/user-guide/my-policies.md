# My Policies (Org Users)

## View assigned policies

1) Open `My Policies` or `Policies to Acknowledge` from the sidebar.
2) Use filters to focus on `Due soon`, `Late`, or specific business units.
3) Click a policy to open details.

## Acknowledge a policy

1) Open the policy detail view.
2) Review the content and metadata (owner, due date, version).
3) Select `Acknowledge` to confirm.

## Track completion

- Completed policies appear in your history or library view.
- Use search and filters to locate a completed policy.
