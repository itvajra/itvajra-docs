# My Policies (Org Users)

## View assigned policies

1) Open `My Policies` or `Policies to Acknowledge` from the sidebar.
2) Use quick filters to focus on `Late`, `Soon`, `Upcoming`, or `Completed`.
3) Use search, category, or business unit filters to narrow results.
4) Click a policy to open details.

## Acknowledge a policy

1) Open the policy detail view.
2) Review the content and metadata (owner, due date, version).
3) Select `Acknowledge` to confirm.

## Track completion

- Completed policies appear in your history or library view.
- Use search and filters to locate a completed policy.

## Tabs and views

- Pending: policies that require acknowledgment
- Library: read-only policies that do not require acknowledgment
- History/Completed: policies you have already acknowledged

## Search and content search

- Search: looks at titles and metadata
- Content search: scans full policy text (slower but more thorough)

## Due dates and status

- Late: due date has passed
- Soon: due date is within the next few days
- Upcoming: due date is farther out or not set
